title: "Far Call"
date: "2025-03-11"
description: "11/03/25 at home, as a dog's howls echoed."
tags: ["poems"]
---

He barked and howled —\
His owner miles far, leaving the hound\
In his house bound.

Barked and howled.

He stared into the town’s light,\
Seeking the whiff of his loved one, too far\
To be found amongst the stars.

\- _11/03/25 at home, as a dog's howls echoed._
